What is SKI-RODEL

SKI-RODEL is the perfect winter outdoors companion.

It’s the ultimate alternative to cross-country skiing. The SKI-RODEL design adds the much needed back support while skiing, and provides a seat for resting, a friend accompanying you, or carries items while traveling.
